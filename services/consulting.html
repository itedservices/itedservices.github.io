Consulting

We can help you match your infrastructure requirements to your students learning requirements. We have experience with SNUP upgrades and being a Project Director of a ICT Cluster, Kelvin has a unique insight into the overall learning requirements of a school.

We are experienced in providing interim solutions to increase your broadband speed while you wait for your school's UFB/RBI connection. Options include VDSL, ADSL load balancing and supply of filtering and caching solutions.

We can offer complete ICT audits that will carefully identify your schools strengths,weaknesses and future challenges. This comprises of a Hardware audit and 360 degree survey of Students, Staff and Community.
