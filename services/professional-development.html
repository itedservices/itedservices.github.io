Professional Development

We love Moodle!

We work extensively with 20+ colleges that use Moodle as their Learning Management System. Because we use this tool every day (Richard and Kelvin still use it for their classes) we are interested in all Moodle based developments and how they can be used to improve learning. This includes finding and testing new Moodle features and blocks with students in real classroom situations.

We are happy to pass this knowledge on, either in half or full day sessions, remotely or one on one if that suits you better. Contact us to organise a PD session or for more information
